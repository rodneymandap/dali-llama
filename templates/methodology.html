Methodology


Data Used
  1. Chlorophyll-a
  2. Colored Dissolved Organic Matter (CDOM)
  3. Normalized Fluorescence Line Height
  4. Sea Surface Temperature
  5. Organic and Inorganic Carbon Particulate (POC and PIC)
  6. Phytoplankton absorption at 443nm wavelength (blue-green algae)
  7. Remote surface reflectance at the green wavelength (531nm+)
Satellites Used
  1. MODIS Aqua and Terra for near real-time (NRT) readings.
  2. Suomi NPP - VIIRS for higher resolution data and cross referencing.
  3. Landsat-8 for testing
